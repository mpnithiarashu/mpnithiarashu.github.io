About

Data has always fascinated me — not just because of numbers, but because every dataset hides a story.

I'm a Data Science & MI Manager with a Master's in Data Science and 10+ years of experience delivering data engineering, BI, and analytics solutions across multinational environments.

Over the years, I've worked across roles including Head of Data, Data Manager, Data Engineer, and Analyst — helping businesses build scalable data platforms, strengthen governance, and turn complex datasets into actionable insights.

Today I'm also an expert in building AI agents — designing autonomous, LLM-powered systems that automate workflows, accelerate analytics, and unlock entirely new ways of working with enterprise data.

I enjoy working at the intersection of technology, business strategy, and storytelling — where data and AI become tools for clarity and better decision-making.

Outside work, I'm passionate about emerging tech, cloud innovation, and experimenting with AI, analytics, and Web3 concepts.
